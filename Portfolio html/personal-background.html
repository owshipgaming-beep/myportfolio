Good Afternoon, I’m Edmon Gutlay Good afternoon, everyone! My name is Edmon Gutlay, I’m 18 years old, and I’d like to share with you a little bit about my life and dreams. I grew up in Project 4, Barangay Marilag, Quezon City. I’m the youngest of seven siblings and was born on June 20, 2007. Life hasn’t always been easy for me, especially after I lost my mother when I was 17 years old. Although we didn’t have much time together, I love her deeply and she remains one of my biggest inspirations in life. I enjoy simple things—playing basketball, riding motorcycles, and eating fruits. I’m not really into sweets or fried food. These small preferences show a bit about who I am: someone who values discipline and keeps a balanced life. My biggest dream is to become a brave and honorable police officer. I want to serve the country, help those in need, and be part of making our society better. After I graduate from Grade 12, I plan to enter the Philippine National Police Academy (PNPA) for training and to begin my journey toward this goal. I know it won’t be easy, but I’m ready to work hard—not just for myself, but for my family, especially my sister, my father, and all those who raised and cared for me. I’m truly grateful to the people who brought me up well and gave me the strength to become who I am today. One day, when I achieve my dreams, I promise to give back and make them proud of the person I’ve become. Thank you so much for listening to my story.
